Pancakes

prep time

30 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Whisk egg in a bowl and add butter, honey and vanilla essence to it. Whisk well.Add mashed banana to it. Mix well.

step 2

Now sift flour and baking powder to the mixture. Mix well to not let any lumps form.Keep adding milk to the mixture while whisking until a smooth paste.

step 3

Now heat a pan and pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.Cook until pancakes are golden brown on both sides.

step 4

Serve hot with sliced banana slices on top.

ingredients

1 Cup All-purpose flour or maida4 tsp White sugar1/2 tsp Baking powderA pinch of Salt1 Egg1 1/2 cup Milk25 ml Vegetable oil1 Banana, mashed1/2 tsp Vanilla essence1 tbsp Honey1 tsp Butter
