Pink Sauce Pasta

prep time

20 min.

cook time

3 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Prepare a marinade with yoghurt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, amchur, kasuri methi, ginger-garlic paste and salt.

step 2

Add the chopped paneer, onion, capsicum and tomato to the marinade. Mix till the veggies and the paneer is coated. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.

step 3

Heat oil in a kadhai, add the marinated mixture and fry it till the paneer turns golden and crisp.

step 4

Spread the green chutney over slices of bread. Place the paneer tikka on the bread and close the sandwich.

step 5

Toast the sandwich on a grill till it is evenly crisp. The paneer tikka sandwich is ready!

ingredients

3/4 cup Yogurt1 tsp Red chilli powder1/4 tsp Turmeric powder1/2 tsp Coriander powder1/4 tsp Cumin powder 1/2 tsp Garam masala1/2 tsp Amchur1 tsp Kasuri methi1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste1/2 tsp Salt1 cup Paneer 1/2 Onion, chopped1/2 Capsicum, chopped1 Tomato, chopped2 tbsp OilBread slices1 cup Green chutney.
