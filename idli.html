Idli

prep time

20 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Take a cup of murmura and wash it and then blend it to a smooth paste. Now to this paste, add rava, salt and curd.

step 2

Make sure that the batter is lump free. Let it rest for a while.

step 3

Now, before cooking and some fruit salt, give it a good whisk and pour this into idli containers.

step 4

Steam and enjoy!

ingredients

1/2 Cup Murmura1/2 cup Rava1 tsp Salt1/2 cup Dahi.
