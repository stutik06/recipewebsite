Banana Cake

prep time

15 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

First mix room temperature butter, and sugar till smooth then add the crushed banana and the milk.

step 2

Once combined, add the flours and baking powder and cinnamon.

step 3

Make sure the batter resembles cake batter (when you lift with a spatula it should fall in 3-5 seconds.)

step 4

Bake at 165 C for 30 mins. Banana cake is ready!

ingredients

210 gm Butter240 gm Brown Sugar150 gm Overripe bananas200 gm Milk300 gm All Purpose Flour 100 gm Almond Flour3 Eggs10 gm Baking Powder2 gm Cinnamon Powder
