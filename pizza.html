Pepperoni Pizza

prep time

20 min.

cook time

3 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Preheat the Pizza oven to 280 degrees. Roll and stretch the dough to a 12-inch round, while creating a rim along the edge of the dough

step 2

Brush olive oil over the edges of pizza, then evenly spread about 2/3 cup of the pizza sauce over pizza (leaving rim uncoated).

step 3

Top evenly with cheese and pepperoni slices; bake 12 - 15 minutes until crust is golden. Remove from oven, cut it into slices.
