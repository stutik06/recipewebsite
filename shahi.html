Shahi Paneer

prep time

45 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Soak the melon seeds in water for about 30 minutes.Grind it to a smooth paste and keep aside.

step 2

In a pan heat butter. Make sure that it does not burn.Shahi Paneer.Add cumin seeds. When they crackle put ginger to it and cook for few seconds.

step 3

Pour tomato puree and bring to a simmer.Shahi Paneer.Now add melon seed paste. Stir well and cook for a minute.

step 4

Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, salt, red chilli powder and green chillis to it.Add some crushed paneer for flavor and bring it to a boil on medium flame while adding water to it.

step 5

Put the paneer cubes and coat them well with gravy. Cook for another one minute.Serve hot, garnished with coriander leaves and butter.

ingredients

1/3 Cup melon seeds, soaked2 tbsp butter1 tsp cumin seeds1 tbsp ginger, chopped1 cup tomato, pureed 1/4 tsp turmeric powder2 tsp coriander powder2 tsp salt1 tsp red chilli powder3 nos green vhilli , choppedcrushed paneer1/2 cup water15 paneer cubes2 tbsp coriander leaves2 butter cubes
