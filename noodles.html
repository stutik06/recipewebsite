Mushroom Noodles

prep time

15 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

5 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Start by boiling noodles al dante, and keep them aside. Heat oil in a wok or kadai or a pan. On a low to medium heat, first add chopped garlic, chopped ginger and chopped green chilies. For a spicy hot tasting noodles, you can increase the quantity of green chilies.

step 2

Saute for a few seconds, then add chopped spring onions. Add both the white and green part. Reserve 2 tablespoon of the greens for garnish.Stir and saute the spring onions till they turn translucent. Then add the sliced mushrooms.

step 3

Stir the mushrooms very well and saute them on a medium to high heat stirring occasionally. The mushrooms will leave water while cooking.6.Continue to saute till all the water dries up and the mushrooms become light golden from the edges.Then add ground black pepper.

step 4

Add soy sauce and stir very well. Add the noodles. Add salt as required.8.Stir and toss the noodles very well. Add rice vinegar or regular white vinegar. Stir and mix. Switch off the heat and lastly add the chopped spring onions greens.

step 5

Give a final stir and serve mushroom noodles plain or with dry veg manchurian or veg manchurian gravy or veg balls in hot garlic sauce.

ingredients

200 gms Noodles300-400 gram Mushrooms1/2 tbsp Chopped Garlic1/2 tsp Chopped ginger1 tsp Chopped green chilies 1/2 tsp Black pepper or white pepper1/2 cup Chopped spring onions 1 to 2 tsp Finely chopped celery (optional) 2 to 2.5tbsp Oil1 tbsp Soya Sauce1 tsp Rice vinegar or regular vinegarSpring onion greens for garnishsalt as required
